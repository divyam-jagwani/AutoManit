About Us

We are second year students from CSE,MANIT. This project was started as a mini project under the guidance of Prof. Narsimha and Prof. Rizwan. But, now it is much more than a mere project to us. Now, we're a team of dedicated students who aim to provide auto service to all the maitians.

Why we are different

We provide cheap, instant and affordable auto service to all the MANITians in need. This project is mainly developed keeping in mind the lack of vehicles and information in the first year of MANIT.

Our vision is to provide E-auto service in MANIT, and gradually to the whole of Bhopal. This step will lead to eco-friendly, cheap and affordable travel. This is in accordance with the government's clean city programme.

Fare Calculation Algorithm

As of now, we have divided our MANIT campus into ten landmarks. And keeping in mind the difference in distance of each landmark with a standard landmark, we calculate the resonable fare for the travel.

Let's say,
Hostel-1 is our standard landmark.
The distance of Hostel-4 from Hostel-1 is (say) 2 units.
The distance of SBI BANK from Hostel-1 is (say) 6 units.
So, the total cost to travel from Hostel-4 to SBI Bank will be= (6-2)*5=Rs.20
Formula: Fare=abs(d2-d1)*5;
